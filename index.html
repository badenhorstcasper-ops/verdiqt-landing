The problem

Most employers lose at the CCMA
before the hearing even starts.

Not because the employee was innocent. Because the procedure was wrong, the charge was defective, or the finding could not withstand scrutiny. Verdiqt closes every one of those gaps.

⚖
Procedural unfairness

A technically guilty employee walks free because notice was defective, representation rights were not properly recorded, or the hearing was not conducted fairly. Verdiqt's guided flow eliminates this entirely.

◫
Weak findings

Generic, copy-pasted outcome letters that collapse under cross-examination at the CCMA. Verdiqt generates structured findings in CCMA award style with relevant case law cited — findings that hold up.

◷
Missed deadlines

The 30-day CCMA referral window catches employers off guard. Verdiqt tracks every dismissal deadline automatically and sends red alerts at day 20 and day 28 — before it is too late.

The solution

Everything a chairperson needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built by a South African labour law specialist. Not generic HR software adapted for SA — purpose-built for the way South African disciplinary hearings actually work.

Guided hearing flow

9-step guided workflow from opening rights to sanction delivery. Checklists, prompts, and suggested wording for every stage. Nothing gets missed.

Legal decision engine

The 2025 Code seven-factor substantive fairness test built in. Generates a risk profile and sanction range based on the evidence. Defensible every time.

AI finding writer

AI drafts findings in CCMA award style with case law citations — Sidumo, Edcon v Pillemer, Avril Elizabeth. You review, edit, and approve. Every draft is watermarked until you sign off.

CCMA deadline tracker

30-day countdown starts automatically on every dismissal. Amber alert at day 20. Red alert at day 28. One-click employer notification. Never miss a referral window again.

Client vault

Upload disciplinary codes, employment contracts, evidence photos, and signed letters per client. Available instantly in every hearing. Scan documents with your phone camera.

Professional documents

Finding reports, outcome letters, and disciplinary records in PDF and Word. Professionally styled. Share via email or WhatsApp directly from the app.
